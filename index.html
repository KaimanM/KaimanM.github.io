About Me

I like to build computers, a lot more than the average person. I have a knack for problem solving and troubleshooting and more often than not will spend the better half of a day, or night, figuring something out. Competitive E-Sports are a strong passion of mine, as are traditional sports. When I was younger I wanted to help develop RuneScape and somehow on that bumpy path I’ve ended up with a Computer Science degree.

My time at university involved me studying many different modules that I thought would be useful and have a solid application in the real world, these modules include but are not limited to; Artificial Intelligence, Mobile Computing, Database Development, Software Engineering and Advanced Object-Oriented Programming.

I also take part in extra curricular activities to sharpen my skills. This includes taking courses from Udemy, Codecademy and learning about various languages from other tutorial sources from around the internet. While I believe that my studies at university introduced me to the world of programming, I understand that what I've learnt is only a drop in the ocean and actively attempt to expand my knowledge.
